about

Over the years, Hacker News has developed somewhat of a reputation for saltiness. Where culinary reviewers once praised the site's bold, forward-thinking approach, they now hold it accountable for the rise of heart disease.

This project seeks to playfully evaluate this tendency using natural language processing and machine learning techniques to rank "saltiness".

Our team is made up of a diverse group of students at Lambda School - about half of our team comes from the Data Science program and the other half from the Full Stack Web Development program.
